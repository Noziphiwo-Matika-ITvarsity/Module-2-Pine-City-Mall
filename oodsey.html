Movie Title: "The Heist of the Century"

Main Actors: Robert De Niro, Anne Hathaway, Tom Hardy Age Restriction: R

A mastermind thief and his skilled team plan an elaborate heist to steal a priceless artifact. As the stakes get higher and the authorities close in, tensions rise, and trust is tested in this thrilling action-packed drama. The meticulously crafted plan begins to unravel as unexpected obstacles arise, forcing the team to adapt and think on their feet. With betrayal lurking around every corner and the pressure mounting, they must navigate a web of deception and danger to pull off the heist of the century. This high-stakes thriller explores themes of loyalty, ambition, and the thin line between right and wrong.
